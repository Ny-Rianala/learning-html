Nesting rules

An open and close tag

There are many rules in writing html. This one is a rule for nesting which is very useful. It is necessary to follow this rules. Whatever you do in html, do not forget to put an open tag and then close it.

Don't do this:

<h1>
List of fruits

The <br> element doen't need an end tag.

Do this instead:

<h1>
List of fruits
<h1>
